Section: 3.1 Intracellular Prdx1 deficiency in acinar cells exacerbates SAP by inducing cell apoptosis and ferroptosis | Paragraph index: 102

Original Text (English, 对照)

Therefore, exploring the behind mechanisms of Prdx1 against SAP become the focus of subsequent work. These results compellingly suggested that promoting endogenous Prdx1 production within acinar cells was a promising therapeutic strategy for improving SAP pathology.
